THE SCIENCE BEHIND BUDDIE

Why create Buddie?

Because more than half of the world’s population unfortunately still doesn’t have access to basic health care. For many more, especially in countries like Nigeria, mental health care is not an option and isn’t given due attention.

How we found a solution!

We put together a team of content gurus, engineers, and designers to combine their love of creating positive content, coding and proffering solutions with the intention of creating a companion (BUDDIE!) who can help people with their mental health.

How to use Buddie

All you have to do is select your preferred buddie (Trevor or Bisola), introduce yourself, and have a conversation. Your buddie has been designed to ask you carefully-crafted questions that will get you closer to living a more happy, fulfilled life.
